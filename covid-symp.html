Symptoms

The most common symptoms include:

  • Chills
  • Fever
  • Sore Throat

Less common symptoms include:

  • Tiredness
  • Headache
  • Sore Eyes
  • Loss of sense of taste or smell
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Dizziness
  • Chest Pain
  • Runny Nose
  • Hoarse Voice
  • Loss of Appetite
  • Nausea and Vomiting
