Treatment

Most people won't need treatment in a hospital. However, if you do show severe symptoms, such as extreme chest pain and having difficulty breathing, you should go to the hospital. Doctors will suggest treatments based on the severity and the risk of it getting worse. They will consider the person’s age and if they have other health problems.
